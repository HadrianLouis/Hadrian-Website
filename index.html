Preface

为什么我要写这本书？不是为了教任何人任何道理。恰恰相反——我是为了让人批评。

我坚信两件事。

第一，只有表达才能获得被指点的机会。你把想法藏在脑子里，没有人能告诉你它哪里错了。你把它写出来、说出来、公开出去，任何一个比你更敏锐的人都能一眼看出你论证里的裂缝。这些裂缝就是你的成长空间。我像一个狼群中的年轻狼，走到最强壮的同伴面前，主动翻过身露出自己的腹部。这不是示弱——这是我知道我的牙齿还不够锋利，我需要那些比我更强的人咬我一口，让我知道我的皮有多薄。

第二，冲突带来进步，一致性带来停滞。当所有人的观点都跟你一样时，你活在回音壁里。回音壁让你很舒服，但回音壁不会让你成长。只有被反驳、被质疑、被迫重新思考你的每一个前提——你才会发现自己到底哪里想得不够清楚。所以我从不畏惧向别人展示自己最激进的观点，甚至故意说一些听起来很狂的话。不是我坚信自己一定对——是我太想知道哪里可能错了。

我渴望错误。错误是认知复利的最大催化剂。你犯了一次错并且承认了它，你就不会犯第二次。你推翻了自己一次，你就比那些从来没审视过自己的人领先了一个维度。所以请你在读本书的时候，不要把它当成什么"正确的答案"来接受。它只是一只狼露出来的肚子。请你咬下去。

Hadrian · 2026年

Why am I writing this book? Not to teach anyone any principles. Quite the contrary—I am writing to be criticized.

I firmly believe in two things.

First, only through expression do you earn the opportunity to be corrected. If you lock your thoughts in your head, no one can tell you where you went wrong. Publish them, speak them, put them out in the open, and anyone sharper than you can spot the fractures in your arguments at a glance. Those fractures are where you grow. I am like a young wolf in a pack, stepping in front of the strongest companion, rolling over to expose my belly. This is not weakness—it is knowing my teeth are not sharp enough yet. I need those who are stronger to bite me, so I know how thin my skin really is.

Second, conflict breeds progress, while uniformity breeds stagnation. When everyone holds the same views as you, you live in an echo chamber. Echo chambers are comfortable, but they do not make you grow. Only by being refuted, questioned, and forced to re-examine every single premise do you find out where your thinking lacks clarity. Therefore, I never hesitate to project my most radical ideas, even deliberately making provocative claims. It is not that I am certain I am right—it is that I am desperate to know where I might be wrong.

I crave errors. Mistakes are the greatest catalyst for cognitive compounding. Make a mistake, acknowledge it, and you will not repeat it. Overturning your own beliefs once puts you a dimension ahead of those who never audit themselves. So as you read this book, do not accept it as some "correct answer." It is merely a wolf exposing its belly. Go ahead and bite.

Hadrian · 2026
